    The Exponential AI story arc

    Every chapter opens by recapping where Exponential AI left off and ends by setting up what happens next.

    Act I — The manual enterprise (Ch 1–3)

    Exponential AI runs on spreadsheets, call centers and human intuition. Machine learning starts predicting demand and churn; deep learning begins to see product images.

    Act II — Language & knowledge (Ch 4–7)

    Transformers unlock language, LLMs answer questions, prompt engineering makes them reliable, and RAG grounds them in Exponential AI's own knowledge to stop hallucinations.

    Act III — Action & coordination (Ch 8–14)

    The AI must act: agents, agentic teams, LangGraph orchestration, and the protocols (MCP, ACP, A2A) plus context engineering that let systems interoperate safely.

    Act IV — Trust, cost & autonomy (Ch 15–20)

    Guardrails, token economics, evaluation harnesses and security harden the platform — until Exponential AI becomes an AI-native, then autonomous, enterprise.
